Privacy Policy

Fuel Log · Version 1.2 · Last updated 11 June 2026

This policy explains what personal data Fuel Log collects, why, and your rights over it. Fuel Log is intended for users aged 18 or over in the United Kingdom and the European Economic Area (EEA).

1. Who we are

The controller responsible for your data is Adrian Richards, trading as Fuel Log ("we", "us"), an individual sole trader established in the United Kingdom.

For any privacy question or to exercise your rights, contact us at fuellogadmin@gmail.com.

4. AI processing (Anthropic)

To estimate calories and nutrition and to tailor coaching suggestions, Fuel Log sends your meal descriptions, workout descriptions, — for workout calorie estimates — your weight and body-fat %, and — if you have set them — your dietary requirements and allergies, to Anthropic, PBC (United States), the provider of the Claude AI model. Your dietary requirements and allergies are sent so the AI can avoid suggesting foods you cannot or prefer not to eat.

No identifiers are sent. Your name, email, and account identifier are never transmitted to Anthropic — the data is sent unlinked to your identity. Full detail is in our sub-processor disclosure.

5. Who we share data with (processors)

We use a small number of service providers who process data on our behalf under contract:

  • Supabase — database hosting and authentication storage (EU region).
  • Cloudflare — serves our application and a lightweight API worker.
  • Google — sign-in (Google OAuth).
  • Anthropic (United States) — AI nutrition/calorie estimation (see §4).

We do not sell your data or share it for advertising. See the full sub-processor list.

6. International transfers

Your data is primarily stored and processed within the UK/EEA. The AI processing in §4 involves a transfer to the United States (Anthropic). Where data leaves the UK/EEA, we rely on the safeguards in Anthropic's Data Processing Addendum: the EU Standard Contractual Clauses (controller-to-processor) and, for UK transfers, the UK Addendum based on the Information Commissioner's approved international data transfer addendum.

7. How long we keep your data

  • We keep your data for as long as your account is active.
  • If you request deletion, we delete your data within 30 days.
  • Accounts with no sign-in for 24 months are flagged and deleted.
  • Deletion removes all of your records together via cascading deletion in our database.

8. Your rights

Under UK and EU GDPR you have the right to: access your data, correct it, request erasure, receive a portable copy, restrict or object to processing, and withdraw consent.

  • Export: use "Download my data" in the app to receive your data as a JSON file.
  • Delete: use "Delete my account" in the app, or follow the deletion instructions.
  • For anything else, email fuellogadmin@gmail.com.

If you are unhappy with how we handle your data you can complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk, or to your local supervisory authority in the EEA.

9. How we protect your data

  • Health data is pseudonymised at the database level and protected by row-level security so each user can only access their own records.
  • Access requires authenticated sign-in (JWT-based).
  • No identifiers are sent to our AI provider.

10. Children

Fuel Log is for adults aged 18 or over and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. We will change the version number and "last updated" date above, and for material changes we will notify you in the app.
