AI processing — Anthropic (United States)

What is sent: the meal description or workout description you enter, and — for workout calorie estimates — your weight and body-fat %. This is used solely to estimate nutrition and calories.

What is NOT sent: we do not send your name, email address, account identifier, or any other identifier. The information is transmitted unlinked to your identity.

Purpose & lawful basis: to provide the app's nutrition/calorie estimation feature, covered by your explicit consent to health-data processing (UK/EU GDPR Art. 9(2)(a)).

International transfer: this involves a transfer to the United States. We rely on the safeguards in Anthropic's Data Processing Addendum, under which Anthropic acts as our processor. It applies the EU Standard Contractual Clauses (Module Two, controller-to-processor; Commission Implementing Decision (EU) 2021/914) and, for UK transfers, the UK Addendum based on the Information Commissioner's approved international data transfer addendum. Under that agreement Anthropic does not train its models on the information we send.
