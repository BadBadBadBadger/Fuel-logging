Delete your account & data

Fuel Log · Last updated 8 June 2026

You can permanently delete your Fuel Log account and all associated data at any time.

In the app (fastest)

  1. Open Fuel Log and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings → Delete my account.
  3. Confirm. Your account and data are deleted immediately, with full removal completed within 30 days.

By email

If you cannot access the app, email fuellogadmin@gmail.com from the Google account you signed up with, asking us to delete your account. We will complete deletion within 30 days and confirm by reply.

What is deleted

DeletedRetained (if any)
Your account, profile (weight, body-fat %, sex), weigh-in history, food/water/workout logs, goals, settings, badges, and history snapshots — all removed via cascading deletion. We keep no copies after deletion. Minimal records may be retained only where required by law (e.g. transaction records, if you ever make a purchase); none apply to a free account.

Questions? fuellogadmin@gmail.com.
